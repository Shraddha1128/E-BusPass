10 Clear Instructions for Using the E-Bus Pass System for College Students

  1. Registration: Visit the official E-Bus Pass website or download the mobile app to register. Provide your student ID, college details, and a valid email address for verification.
  2. Document Submission: Upload required documents, including a recent passport-size photo, valid college ID, and proof of enrollment. Ensure documents are clear and legible for faster approval.
  3. Form Completion: Fill out the online application form, including personal information, contact details, and preferred bus routes. Double-check the information for accuracy before submitting.
  4. Payment: Select the appropriate pass category (monthly, quarterly, or annual). Make the payment using your preferred payment method (debit card, credit card, UPI, or net banking).
  5. Confirmation: After submission and payment, you’ll receive a confirmation email with your pass details. Your digital E-Bus Pass will be generated within 24-48 hours.
  6. Digital Pass Usage: Download your E-Bus Pass from the website or app. Carry it on your smartphone or print it out for easy scanning when boarding the bus.
  7. Bus Entry: Present your digital or printed pass to the bus conductor or scan it at the designated machine upon entry.
  8. Renewal: Renew your pass before it expires by logging into your account and selecting the "Renew Pass" option. Update any necessary information and make the renewal payment.
  9. Lost or Damaged Pass: If your pass is lost or damaged, report it on the website or app. Apply for a duplicate pass with minimal charges.
  10. Support: For any issues or queries, contact customer support through the helpline number or email provided on the website/app.
